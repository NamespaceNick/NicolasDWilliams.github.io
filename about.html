As a kid with ADHD, games kept my attention and offered me a judgement -free medium where I could ask a million questions and learn everything about the world I'd been placed in. Somehow it wasn't until freshman year of college that I realized that I could help contribute to these worlds.

To enrich my understanding of the field, and in hopes of helping others create experiences of their own, I became a student instructor for a senior design course centered around video game development.
